Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

This site uses privacy-friendly analytics to understand how visitors use our content. We collect:

  • Pages visited and navigation paths
  • Referring sources, such as which site linked to us
  • Browser type, device type, and screen size
  • Country-level location derived from a privacy-preserving hash; we do not store your IP address
  • Interactions with page elements, such as game and button clicks

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personally identifiable information
  • Precise location data
  • Data that tracks you across other websites

How we collect it

We use PostHog, an analytics platform configured in cookieless, anonymous mode. No cookies or persistent identifiers are stored in your browser, you are not tracked across sessions or devices, and no individual user profiles are created. Because analytics does not use cookies, no analytics cookie consent banner is shown.

How we use it

Analytics data is used solely to understand which games are useful, identify broken pages or navigation issues, and measure overall traffic trends. We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

Third-party access

Aggregated, anonymous event data is processed by PostHog, Inc. PostHog's infrastructure is located in the United States and PostHog provides GDPR compliance controls. Read PostHog's privacy policy and GDPR compliance documentation for more detail.

Your rights

Because we collect no personally identifiable information and create no user profiles, there is no individual profile data to access, correct, or delete. For privacy questions, contact adamabdelhamed@live.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
